Our Story

Built with a single goal — to bring structured, high-quality chess education into Hong Kong schools and make it accessible to every child.

Today, we combine academic methodology, professional coaching, and modern digital tools to create one of the most effective chess learning systems in Hong Kong.

From our Central academy to classrooms across Hong Kong Island and Kowloon, we serve players from their very first move to FIDE-rated tournament competition.
